Frequently Asked Questions

What if my PDF statement is scanned or not reading?
V-Import is designed for original digital PDFs from the bank. If you have a scanned image or a format that isn't supported, don't worry! Simply copy-paste your data into our Universal Excel Template, and V-Import will still automate the Tally entry for you.
Do I need internet to use it?
No! V-Import is 100% Offline. Your client data never leaves your computer. Internet is only required for the initial license activation.
Does it work with all Tally versions?
It is optimized for Tally Prime (Direct Integration). For older Tally ERP 9 versions, the software can generate XML files that you can import manually.
